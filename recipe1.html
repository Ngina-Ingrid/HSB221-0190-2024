🍲 Kenyan Beef Stew

🛒 Ingredients

  • 500g beef chunks (preferably boneless)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 1 green pepper, diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 potatoes, cubed
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 cup water or beef broth

👩🏽‍🍳 Instructions

  1. In a large saucepan, heat oil over medium heat. Sauté onions until translucent.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, stir for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add beef chunks and brown on all sides (about 5–7 minutes).
  4. Stir in tomatoes and tomato paste. Cook until soft and blended.
  5. Add paprika, curry powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  6. Add potatoes, carrots, green pepper, and 1 cup water or broth.
  7. Cover and simmer on low heat for 35–40 minutes or until beef is tender and veggies are cooked.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice, ugali, or chapati.
