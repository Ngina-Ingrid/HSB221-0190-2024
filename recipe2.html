🥥 Creamy Coconut Chicken Curry

🛒 Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 red chili (optional), sliced
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • 1 tomato, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Juice of 1/2 a lime

👩🏽‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan. Sauté onions until soft and golden.
  2. Add garlic, ginger, and chili. Cook for 1–2 minutes.
  3. Add chicken and sear until golden on all sides.
  4. Stir in curry powder, turmeric, cumin, and salt. Cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add grated tomato and cook for 5 minutes until thickened.
  6. Pour in the coconut milk and add green pepper. Simmer uncovered for 20–25 minutes until creamy.
  7. Finish with lime juice and fresh coriander. Serve with rice or naan.
